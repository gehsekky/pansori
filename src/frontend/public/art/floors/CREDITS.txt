Local-room / town floor textures — seamless top-down ground tiles, four
types × three variants (grass, dirt, cobblestone, sand), 128x128. The
engine picks one variant per cell deterministically (FLOOR_VARIANTS).

All four types are now COMMERCIAL-CLEAN (CC0 + original) — see the
per-source breakdown below. This pack previously used arexxuru's "Pixel
Floor Texture Pack" (non-commercial only), which forced the whole
project to be non-commercial; it has been fully replaced.

grass_{1,2,3}.png, cobblestone_{1,2,3}.png
  From the "Tiny Texture Pack" (128x128) by Screaming Brain Studios
  (https://screamingbrainstudios.itch.io/tiny-texture-pack). Released
  under CC0 / Public Domain — usable in any project commercial or not,
  with or without credit, no restrictions.

dirt_{1,2,3}.png, sand_{1,2,3}.png
  ORIGINAL procedural textures generated by
  src/frontend/scripts/gen-floor-dirt-sand.py (tileable value-noise →
  seamless). The SBS pack has no earth or fine-sand texture, so these are
  generated in-project rather than pulled from a second third-party pack.
  Original work — no third-party license applies.
