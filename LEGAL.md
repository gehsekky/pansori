## Painted-art overlay (separate repository)

This repository ships a **free, fully-playable art tier** built only from
redistributable assets — the game-icons.net font above (glyphs), plus the CC0 /
original floor textures and per-terrain color tints. pansori's richer **painted
tier** uses licensed raster packs that are licensed for use **in** the game but
**not for standalone redistribution**, so they are **not included in this repo**.
They live in a separate private repository,
[pansori-assets](https://github.com/gehsekky/pansori-assets), and are overlaid at
build/deploy time (see [ASSETS.md](ASSETS.md)); per-file provenance is recorded in
that repo's `CREDITS.txt` files. The packs and their creators, credited here:

- **Overland terrain tiles** + **location markers** —
  [David Baumgart](https://dgbaumgart.com) ("Tile Basic Terrain Set", "Tile
  Medieval Fantasy Locations"). Usable in a free or commercial game, may be
  modified/remixed, not repackaged for standalone distribution or sale.
- **Inventory item icons** — [David Baumgart](https://dgbaumgart.com) ("Icons:
  Medieval Arms & Armor") and
  [Vivid Motion Studio](https://vivid-motion-assets.itch.io/) ("Animated RPG
  Icons Ultimate Kit", static single-frame variants). Usable in any commercial or
  personal game, may be modified/recolored/scaled; the raw sprites may not be
  resold or redistributed as a standalone asset pack.
- **Party / NPC map sprites** —
  ["Tiny Swords"](https://pixelfrog-assets.itch.io/tiny-swords) by Pixel Frog.
  Usable in free or commercial projects and may be modified; the assets may not
  be redistributed, resold, or repackaged on their own (even if modified) —
  which is why pansori's derived sprite crops live in the private overlay, not here.
